# Healing

Recovering HP during and after combat.

## Sources of healing

- **Hit dice** (short rest): spend a hit die, roll it + CON modifier, regain that much HP. Once spent, you don't recover it until long rest.
- **Spells**: Cure Wounds (1d8+mod), Healing Word (bonus action, 1d4+mod), Prayer of Healing (long rest only), etc.
- **Class features**: Paladin's Lay on Hands (pool of HP, action), Druid's Healing Spirit (concentration, 1d6 per creature).
- **Potions of Healing**: 2d4+2 HP (common), 4d4+4 (greater), 8d4+8 (superior), 10d4+20 (supreme).
- **Long rest**: restores all HP.

## When healing matters most

- **Mid-combat**: only useful for preventing deaths or enabling a tank to keep fighting. Generally, dealing damage wins faster than healing.
- **Between combats**: short rests and hit dice matter most here.
- **After a tough day**: long rest restores everything.

## Player tips

- **Healing Word > Cure Wounds**: as a bonus action, Healing Word lets you stabilize an ally while still using your action to attack.
- **Don't out-heal damage**: in a tough fight, healing is a band-aid. Kill the enemy faster.
- **Long-rest between dungeons**: don't enter a new dungeon at half HP.
