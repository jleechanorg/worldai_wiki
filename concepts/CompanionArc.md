# Companion Arc

A companion's personal storyline that runs alongside the main campaign.

## Structure

1. **Introduction**: companion joins the party (early in the campaign).
2. **Personal quest hook**: the companion mentions a personal goal.
3. **Side content**: optional scenes that develop the companion's story.
4. **Crisis moment**: a choice that tests the companion's values.
5. **Resolution**: the companion's arc concludes (positively or negatively).

## Approval system

Companions track approval of player choices:
- +approval: choices that align with the companion's values.
- -approval: choices that violate them.
- High approval: bonus scenes, romance options, special items.
- Low approval: companion may leave or betray the party.

## Romance

Some companions can be romanced:
- Triggered by high approval + specific scene choices.
- Romance is opt-in. Players who don't want romance can play through without it.

## Player tips

- **Engage with companion scenes**: they add depth to the campaign.
- **Check approval**: if a companion's approval drops too low, they may leave.
- **Read the companion's bio**: their personality tells you what they value.
