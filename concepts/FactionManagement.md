# Faction Management

The player-facing controls for managing your faction.

## Resources

- **Gold**: hire troops, bribe, buy equipment.
- **Influence**: political capital. Spend on diplomatic actions.
- **Materials**: build fortifications, craft equipment.

## Member roster

Each named member has:
- Name, level, class
- Role (combat, scout, leader, support)
- Relationships to other members
- Current location / assignment

## Territory

Territories you control have:
- Defensive strength
- Economic output (gold per turn)
- Strategic value (intel targets)

## Diplomatic relations

Track relationships with other factions:
- Allied / Friendly / Neutral / Hostile / At War

Diplomacy takes influence and time. Alliances are powerful but fragile.
