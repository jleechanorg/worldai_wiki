# Faction Intel

The information-gathering subsystem of the faction minigame. Higher intel = better combat outcomes.

## How intel works

1. **Send scouts**: declare which rival faction to scout.
2. **Cost**: each intel operation costs time and may cost resources.
3. **Result**: success gives you intel on the rival's members, resources, defenses.

## What intel gives you

- **Combat bonus**: each point of intel grants a modifier to your faction battle roll.
- **Strategic info**: you learn the rival's weaknesses, plans, and leadership.
- **Risk reduction**: attacks with high intel have lower casualty rates.

## Player tips

- **Always scout before attacking**: it's cheap and gives huge combat bonuses.
- **Decoy intel**: send fake scouts to mislead the rival.
- **Counter-intel**: defend against enemy scouts (costs resources).
