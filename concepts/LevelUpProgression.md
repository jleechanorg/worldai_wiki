# Level Up Progression

Long-term level progression patterns from real campaigns. Use these as reference for planning your own.

## Two case studies

### Itachi V2 — slow escalation

- **Total scenes**: 432
- **Final level**: 44
- **Scenes per level**: ~10
- **XP grants**: distributed across combat, quest completion, milestone achievements

The Itachi V2 campaign used the standard 5e XP curve. The player leveled once every ~10 scenes on average, with some clusters (early levels happen fast) and some plateaus (mid-campaign pacing).

### Aristocrat V2 — fast escalation

- **Total scenes**: 50
- **Final level**: 44
- **Scenes per level**: ~1.1
- **XP grants**: front-loaded by reincarnation blessing

The Aristocrat V2 campaign started with "Level 10 Divine Blessings" — the reincarnation setup gave the PC a head start. By scene 10, the PC was already L20+. By scene 50, L44.

## Two patterns

### Slow escalation (Itachi V2)

Best for:
- Realistic drama
- Combat matters at every level
- Time to grow into each tier of play

Pacing tip: aim for ~10 scenes per level. Some levels (early ones) come faster; later levels come slower.

### Fast escalation (Aristocrat V2)

Best for:
- Mythic / god-tier arcs
- Power fantasy
- Compressed timelines

Pacing tip: front-load the power via setup ("divine blessing," "ancient artifact," "inherited throne"). Then run the escalation curve.

## Beyond level 20

Both case studies reached level 44. The system supports up to level 50+ via:
- **Epic levels** (21+): custom progression tables.
- **Mythic levels** (30+): divine / planar power.
- **Transcendent levels** (40+): reality-shaping.

The Itachi V2 "Emperor of Eternal Stillness" arc was a transcendent-level ending — the PC was a god-tier entity reshaping the multiverse.

## Level-up modal

At each level-up, a modal appears. The player must complete it before play continues. See [[concepts/LevelUp]] for the modal details.

For fast-escalation campaigns, the modal can feel repetitive. Tip: if your campaign is fast-escalation, plan your character choices ahead of time so each modal is quick.

## Player tips

- **Plan your ASI levels**: 4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 19th. Decide feats vs +2 ASI ahead of time.
- **Plan your subclass choice**: at 1st, 2nd, or 3rd depending on class.
- **Don't skip the modal**: it pauses narration, but completing it unlocks the next tier.
- **Use THINK: prompts for meta-strategy**: useful for power-progression planning.
