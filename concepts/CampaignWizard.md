# Campaign Wizard

The 3-step flow for creating a new campaign.

## Step 1 — Setting

Pick or describe the world:
- **Pre-built settings**: Naruto, Game of Thrones, BG3, custom fantasy, etc.
- **Custom setting**: free-text description of your world.

The setting determines:
- Available races/classes (or setting-equivalents)
- Lore, geography, factions
- Tone defaults

## Step 2 — Character

Build your character:
- **AI-generated**: see [[concepts/CharacterCreation]] Path 1.
- **Hand-rolled**: see [[concepts/CharacterCreation]] Path 2.

The character determines:
- Starting stats, class, subclass
- Equipment
- Personality / backstory hooks

## Step 3 — Companion

Optionally, add a starting companion:
- **None**: solo campaign.
- **AI-generated**: the system generates a companion.
- **Choose from roster**: pick from a list of pre-built companions for the setting.

Some settings (like Game of Thrones) have rich companion rosters. Others (custom settings) start solo.

## After the wizard

The campaign starts in Scene 1. The GM narrates the opening based on your setting + character + companion choices, and prompts you for your first action.

## Player tips

- **Take your time on setting**: the setting shapes everything. A Naruto campaign feels different from a BG3 campaign.
- **AI-generate on your first run**: let the system show you what it can do.
- **Add a companion**: solo campaigns can be lonely. Companions add depth.
