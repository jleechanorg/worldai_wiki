# Companion Personality

How companion NPCs are characterized.

## MBTI types

The system uses MBTI types as shorthand for personality:
- **I/E**: introvert / extrovert (energy direction)
- **S/N**: sensing / intuitive (information gathering)
- **T/F**: thinking / feeling (decision-making)
- **J/P**: judging / perceiving (lifestyle)

Example: an INTJ companion is introverted, intuitive, thinking, judging — a strategic planner who keeps to themselves.

## Alignment

D&D-style alignments:
- **Lawful Good / Neutral Good / Chaotic Good**: heroic
- **Lawful Neutral / True Neutral / Chaotic Neutral**: pragmatic
- **Lawful Evil / Neutral Evil / Chaotic Evil**: villainous

## Why MBTI + alignment

These labels give the LLM a quick shorthand for "how does this character react in this situation?" instead of having to remember detailed backstory for every NPC.

## Companion arcs

Companions have story arcs:
- Personal quest: each companion has a personal goal (find their lost sister, clear their name, etc.).
- Approval: companions approve or disapprove of player choices. High approval unlocks bonus scenes.
- Romance: some companions have romance arcs (player choice to pursue or not).

See [CompanionArc](CompanionArc.md), [NPCRelationships](NPCRelationships.md).
