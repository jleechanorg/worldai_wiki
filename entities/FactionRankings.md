# Faction Rankings

How factions are ranked against each other. Higher rank = more visibility = more rivals.

## How ranking works

Each faction has a numerical power score ([FactionPower](../concepts/FactionPower.md)) and a rank derived from it. The top faction is rank 1.

## Why rank matters

- **Visibility**: top-ranked factions attract more attacks.
- **Recruitment**: top factions can recruit better named NPCs.
- **Diplomacy**: top factions have more diplomatic leverage.
- **Economy**: top factions have better economic output.

## What changes ranking

- **Combat victories**: defeating a higher-ranked rival moves you up.
- **Combat defeats**: losing to a lower-ranked rival moves you down.
- **Resource growth**: more members + more territory = higher rank.
- **Time**: rank decays if you don't actively grow.

## Player tips

- **Climb steadily**: don't skip ranks. Each rank has its own challenges.
- **Defend your rank**: top-ranked factions attract rivals.
- **Build before climbing**: don't attack a higher-ranked faction until you're ready.
