# D&D 5e Rules

WorldArchitect.AI runs primarily on D&D 5th Edition rules (SRD-derived). The full 5e system is supported for default settings; custom settings adapt the rules spine.

## Core rules reference

The full D&D 5e rules are available at the official SRD (System Reference Document) — free to use.

## What's not from 5e

The faction minigame is custom to WorldArchitect.AI — not part of standard D&D 5e. The 9 directive categories for god mode are also custom.

Custom settings (Naruto, Game of Thrones, BG3, isekai) adapt the 5e spine to their lore.

## Player tips

- **Read the SRD if you want to learn 5e**: it's free.
- **Use this wiki as a quick-reference**: each concept page links to relevant SRD topics.
- **Custom settings adapt 5e**: Naruto's "Sharingan" is a class feature; Game of Thrones' "house allegiances" are background features.
