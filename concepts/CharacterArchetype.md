# Character Archetype

A shorthand for the role a character plays in the party.

## Tank

Front-line fighters who absorb damage:
- **Fighter** (Champion, Battle Master)
- **Barbarian** (Berserker, Totem Warrior)
- **Paladin** (Oath of the Ancients, Oath of Devotion)

High HP, high AC, draw enemy attention.

## Striker

High-burst damage dealers:
- **Rogue** (Assassin, Arcane Trickster)
- **Ranger** (Hunter, Gloom Stalker)
- **Monk** (Way of the Open Hand)

Big single-target damage. Often rely on positioning.

## Controller

Area-denial and battlefield manipulation:
- **Wizard** (Evoker, Abjurer)
- **Sorcerer** (Draconic Bloodline, Wild Magic)
- **Druid** (Circle of the Land)
- **Warlock** (Great Old One)

Crowd control, AoE damage, environmental effects.

## Support

Healing and buffing:
- **Cleric** (Life Domain, Light Domain)
- **Bard** (Lore College, Glamour College)
- **Paladin** (Oath of the Crown — yes, paladins tank AND support)

Healing, buffs, debuffs, utility.

## Hybrid

Many builds are hybrids:
- Paladin: tank + support
- Ranger: striker + controller
- Warlock: striker + controller
