# Daemon (Prince Daemon)

A high-level antagonist archetype from a published campaign example. Daemon is a charming, dangerous antagonist — confident, witty, and willing to use violence.

## Personality

- **MBTI**: ENTJ (or similar — commanding, strategic)
- **Alignment**: Chaotic Neutral leaning toward villain
- **Voice**: witty, dangerous, occasionally philosophical
- **Goals**: power, legacy, freedom from constraint

## How Daemon-like characters work in the system

NPCs with personality tags (MBTI, alignment) have:
- Predictable behavior patterns
- Voice consistent with their tags
- Decisions aligned with their stated goals

When the player interacts with such an NPC, the dialog agent uses the tags as a semantic anchor.

## Player tips

- **High-charisma NPCs are persuasive**: don't trust their first offer.
- **Antagonists with MBTI tags** are predictable but not simple.
- **Build relationships**: even antagonists can become allies if their goals align with yours.
