# Level Up

When your character gains enough XP, they level up. Leveling grants:
- **HP increase** (rolled hit die + CON modifier, or fixed value)
- **Class features** (at certain levels)
- **Subclass features** (at certain levels)
- **ASI or feat** (at 4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 19th for most classes)
- **Spell slots** (for casters)

## The level-up modal

When you cross an XP threshold, a level-up modal appears. You must make choices before play continues:
- **ASI or feat** (at ASI levels)
- **Subclass** (at subclass levels)
- **Spell selection** (for prepared casters)
- **Fighting style** (for Fighters at 2nd)
- **Other class-specific choices**

The modal locks narration until you've completed it. This prevents the GM from narrating post-level-up actions before you've made your choices.

## Player tips

- **Know your ASI levels**: 4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 19th. Plan your build ahead of time.
- **Don't delay**: leveling gives you power. Don't bench XP.
- **Read your new features**: when you level, take a moment to understand your new abilities.
