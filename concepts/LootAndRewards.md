# Loot & Rewards

What you get for completing encounters, quests, and milestones.

## XP

Awarded for:
- Defeating enemies in combat (varies by enemy CR)
- Completing quests
- Reaching milestones (story beats, exploration, social)

XP accumulates. When you cross a threshold, you level up ([LevelUp](LevelUp.md)).

## Gold

Awarded for:
- Defeating enemies (some drop gold)
- Finding treasure
- Completing quests
- Selling loot

Gold is used for:
- Buying equipment ([Equipment](Equipment.md))
- Hiring services (NPCs, transport)
- Maintaining lifestyle (downtime activities)

## Items

- **Common**: mundane but useful (rope, torches, basic weapons).
- **Uncommon**: magical effects (e.g., +1 weapons, Bag of Holding).
- **Rare**: powerful (e.g., Flame Tongue, Winged Boots).
- **Very Rare**: campaign-defining (e.g., Holy Avenger, Vorpal Sword).
- **Legendary**: artifacts (e.g., Excalibur, Hand of Vecna).
- **Artifact**: world-shaping (e.g., One Ring).

Items have attunement limits (typically 3 attuned items at once).

## Player tips

- **Loot everything**: hidden caches are common in dungeons.
- **Sell common loot**: it adds up.
- **Identify before using**: cursed items are a thing. Use the Identify spell.
- **Attune strategically**: don't waste attunement slots on situational items.

See [LevelUp](LevelUp.md), [Equipment](Equipment.md).
